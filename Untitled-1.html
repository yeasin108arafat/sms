Mentions Formula

The total number of content generated across online, social or print media.

All Media = Some of all the medias total mentions.

Instagram Stories = The total number of IG story frames posted tagging your brand by our MVO influencers.

Instagram Posts = The total number of IG posts generated tagging your brand by our MVO influencers.

TikTok = The total number of TikToks generated tagging your brand by our MVO TikTokers.

Online Media = Total number of articles tracked mentioning your brand across news sites and blogs.

Print Media = The total number of brand/product mentions in print media.

Contributors

83

Contributors Formula

The total number of authors generating content across any media.

All Media = Sum of all the medias contributors.

Instagram Stories = The total number of IG story authors mentioning your brand.

Instagram Posts = The total number of IG posts authors mentioning your brand.

TikTok = The total number of TikTokers tagging your brand.

Online Media = Total number of online publications mentioning your brand.

Print Media = The total number of print publications mentioning your brand.

Reach

17.3M

Reach Formula

The estimated number of unique people your content could have reached.

All Media = Sum of all the medias reach

Instagram Stories = Followers

Instagram Posts = Followers

TikTok = Views

Online Media = Unique Monthly Visitors

Print Media = Readership

Impressions

31.5M

Impressions Formula

The number of times a given mention could be viewed.

All Media = Sum of all the medias impressions

Instagram Stories = Number of mentions x Followers

Instagram Posts = Number of mentions x Followers

TikTok = Total Views

Online Media = Number of mentions x Unique monthly visitors

Print Media = Number of mentions x Readership

PR Value

$1.3M

PR Value Formula

Using a multiple to calculate the added value of generating editorial style coverage over standard advertisements. PR Value = 3 x Ad Value

All Media = Sum of all the medias PR values.

Instagram Stories = Ad value based on their Followers using a CPM of $20.

Instagram Posts = Ad value based on the account’s Followers using a CPM of $20.

TikTok = Ad value based on the video’s Views using a CPM of $40.

Online Media = Ad value based on the site’s Unique monthly visitors using a CPM of $10.

Print Media = Ad value based on the Size of the mention using the publication’s full page rate.

Sponsored Coverage

Social coverage that contains the below terms, is labelled as sponsored coverage.

  • ad OR advert OR advertisement
  • brandedcontent
  • gifted
  • #paid
  • paid partnership OR paidpartnership
